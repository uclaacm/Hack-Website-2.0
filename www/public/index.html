What is Hack?

We are a student-run organization that aims to empower UCLA students to influence their world through code. We host events for coders of all skill levels. So whether you've been to 10 hackathons, or you just learned "hello world", we're happy to have you.

HackSchool

HackSchool is a quarter-long course that gives beginners the skills they need to build and deploy their own projects. You will learn from experienced teachers, collaborate on group projects, and meet like-minded peers. Our schedule for this year is Web Development in the Fall, Android in the Winter, and iOS in the Spring.
